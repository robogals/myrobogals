What's the Dashboard?

The Dashboard will soon fill with various cards containing an overview of your involvement in Robogals.

Cards will include items such as upcoming activities, goal progress of chapters, and new ideas for workshops.

If you have a suggestion for something you'd like to see on the Dashboard, please drop it by Robogals Support.

What's with the weird user image?

We're now supporting Gravatars on your primary email address.

To change your image, simply sign up for a Gravatar, and your user image will change automatically on your next login to myRobogals beta.

Eh... How do I log out?

Tap your name or user image in the main menu to reveal the accounts menu.
