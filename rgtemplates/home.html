myRobogals is our new global intranet and record-keeping tool, intended to simplify many of Robogals' day-to-day tasks including organising school visits, maintaining a member database, communicating with members, storing records reliably for future generations and easily collecting statistics on a global scale.

myRobogals is in beta testing. It currently supports the following features:
  • View/update your profile
  • View/update your chapter
  • Maintain a database of members
  • Maintain a database of schools
  • Maintain a database of school visits
  • Students can join Robogals online
  • Send email to all members in chapter
  • Send SMS to all members in chapter
  • RSVP online for school visits
New features coming soon:
  • Files manager
  • Wiki
  • Online discussion forum - with separate boards for your chapter's committee only, your chapter's members only, and all members globally
  • And more...
Throughout these early stages, we really appreciate any feedback, suggestions and other queries! Just email my@robogals.org to get in touch with the myRobogals development team. {% endblock %}
